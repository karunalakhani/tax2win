Due Dates for Compliances



When it comes to income tax, there are a number of mandatory tax formalities that must be completed within the specified due dates/ deadlines, for example, filing income tax returns and paying advance tax on time, and so on.

Here is the TAX CALENDAR:

Description Nature of Deductions
15th June First instalment of advance tax for the Financial Year
31st July Filing Income Tax Returns for the Financial Year (FY) 2021-22 – Individuals and Firms who are not subject to a tax audit and have not engaged in any international or specified domestic transactions
15th Sept Second instalment of advance tax for the Financial Year
30th Sept Under Section 44AB - Submission of audit report – Taxpayers eligible for audit under the Income Tax Act
31st October ITR filing for taxpayers who need to be audited (not having international or specified domestic transactions)
31st October Submission of audit report – For Taxpayers with transfer pricing and specified domestic transactions
30th November ITR filing for taxpayers who need to be audited – Having international or specified domestic transactions.
15th December The third instalment of advance tax
31st December The last day to file a revised or a belated return for Financial Year
15th March
  • i. The fourth advance tax instalment payment for the Financial Year.
  • ii. The due date for the entire amount of advance tax for FY 2020-21 for taxpayers covered by Section 44AD and 44ADA's presumptive scheme."
